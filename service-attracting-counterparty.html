Attract a counterparty

The service is aimed at finding and attracting a foreign business partner, buyer or seller of goods or services in accordance with the needs of the customer.

What we do:

  • We agree on the requirements for the counterparty, the parameters of the desired transaction.
  • We carry out a search, use our network of partners in the country, trade missions of foreign states, various business associations and unions.
  • We connect the interests of the Customer and Counterparties, filtering unpromising contacts.
  • We provide contact details to the Customer, help organize meetings and negotiations, taking into account national characteristics, in order to conclude an agreement.
  • We monitor the progress of the execution of the contract, help to resolve emerging problems.

Price:

It depends on the tasks to be solved in the order, it may consist of a fixed fee, a monthly fee, a percentage of the parameters of the transaction, the volume, the result obtained, etc.

Restrictions:

Insufficient experience of the Customer in the implementation of foreign economic activity. Possible solution - trade agency service
