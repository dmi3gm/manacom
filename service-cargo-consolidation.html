Cargo consolidation

Reception of goods from various Chinese suppliers at our warehouse in Xi'an.

There are different ways to ship goods from China. All of them differ in cost, terms of execution and terms of delivery.

Sometimes the delivery of one small-sized cargo is not rational and it is more profitable to combine several orders from different suppliers or separated by the time of receipt into one delivery

We will help to combine several small-sized cargoes from one or several suppliers in one place (warehouse), temporarily store cargoes up to the optimal delivery volume and send them along a pre-planned route.

Upon acceptance of the goods to the warehouse, we can check its quality according to the requirements of the customer, this will reduce the cost of returning low-quality goods from another country.

Our warehouse in the bonded area can be used for goods that have passed customs clearance.

We can provide the service of technical export of cargo arriving at our regular warehouse - to buy products from the customer's suppliers.

We ship cargo by air, railway, sea and road transport.

The cost of consolidation depends on many components, the cost is calculated after receiving full information about the cargo, quantity, regularity of delivery.
