Marketing research

This is one of the first steps in preparing for the implementation of international trade transactions. It is necessary to collect and analyze information on sales of competing products in the country of interest. The availability of such information increases the effectiveness of future negotiations and relieves the customer of deceived expectations.

Information can be collected from various sources:

  • Collection of information from open sources
  • Marketing research in the country of destination
  • Official data of the Government (Trade missions, etc.)​
  • Search and survey of potential contractors

What we do:

  • We agree on ways to obtain information
  • We obtain the missing information, use our network of partners in the country, contact potential counterparties, hire subcontractors, conduct an analysis,
  • We determine the indicative price and estimated sales volumes of products in the country. Running parameters of quality, packaging, taste, packaging and other features.​
  • We provide information to the Customer.
