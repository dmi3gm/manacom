Reliability Check

Verification of a foreign counterparty is a mandatory step in the preparation of an international transaction.

When concluding contracts with foreign counterparties, additional risks arise:

  • such a counterparty is more difficult to hold liable for failure to fulfill obligations
  • the litigation will be more costly, especially if you have to conduct a dispute in a foreign court
  • various methods of doing business​

If the counterparty turns out to be dishonest or a member of the sanctions lists, the company will suffer significant losses.

What we do:

We receive information from paid and open sources in China and Russia, we form a certificate in the language of the customer. We give explanations on the content and focus on potential risks. The cost of preparing such information is 1000 CNY.

Offline verification of a potential counterparty with a visit to the office or production costs 3500 CNY + travel expenses.
