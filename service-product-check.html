Product check

Pre-shipment inspection is an important step in the quality control process before your shipment is shipped.

It is carried out after the completion of production, when 100% of the batch is produced and at least 80% is packed.

It gives you the opportunity to make sure that the goods intended for shipment are manufactured according to your specification, the original requirements.

Conducted based on customer requirements

Countries:

China, Russia

Steps:

Arrival of the inspector at the factory

The inspector arrives when production has started and there are already some finished products

Quantity check

The inspector recounts shipping cartons to check their exact quality

Random sampling

Boxes are randomly selected for subsequent inspection to assess the overall quality of the lot

External and internal inspection

The inspector carefully checks the samples for visual defects

Compliance check

Our inspector evaluates whether the product meets your requirements

Check functionality and security

Our inspector carefully checks the samples for functionality and safety

Inspection report

Our inspector prepares a report that reflects the results of the inspection and includes photos and videos
