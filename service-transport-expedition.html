transport expedition

We organize the delivery of your goods from China or Russia

We will calculate the optimal route for the delivery of products, taking into account the timing, cost, and measures of state support.

We operate with various modes of transport (sea, rail, road, air) depending on the cargo, quantity, requirements for storage and transportation conditions, time limits or transportation costs.

We work with dangerous and non-standard cargo.

We will take into account the current state of logistics facilities: access roads, transshipment points, ports, checkpoints, their location and performance.

Operating with all these factors, complete with established relationships with transport companies, we will organize the optimal cost of delivery.

For the buyer, we can inspect the purchased goods.

The cost of transportation depends on many components, the cost is calculated after receiving full information about the cargo being transported and the points of departure and receipt.
